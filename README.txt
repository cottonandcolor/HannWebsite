HANNAH MARA REAL ESTATE WEBSITE
================================

This package combines Iteration 2 (personal brand and services) with Iteration 3
(listings and portfolio).

FILES
-----
index.html                  Main website
assets/styles.css           Layout, branding and mobile design
assets/script.js            Mobile menu, property filters, modal and contact form

assets/806-clearwell.svg     Clearly labeled listing-photo placeholder
assets/jarrell-land.svg      Clearly labeled land-photo placeholder
assets/commercial-portfolio.svg Commercial portfolio placeholder

HOW TO PREVIEW
--------------
Open index.html in Chrome, Edge, Safari or Firefox.

BEFORE PUBLISHING
-----------------
1. Replace the placeholder headshot with Hannah's approved white floral top headshot.
2. Replace the temporary vector wordmark with the exact approved Full Circle Real Estate logo.
3. Replace all property placeholders with real property photographs only.
4. Confirm current price, status, facts and availability for every featured property.
5. Add live links for:
   - Information About Brokerage Services
   - TREC Consumer Protection Notice
   - Privacy policy
6. Connect the contact form to the preferred CRM, website form provider or email service.
   The preview currently opens a pre-filled email to Hannah.
7. Add a domain, SSL certificate, analytics and spam protection.
8. Confirm brokerage and TREC advertising compliance before publication.

EASY IMAGE REPLACEMENT
----------------------
The simplest method is to convert the approved images to JPG or PNG and update these
image paths in index.html:
- assets/hannah-headshot.svg
- assets/full-circle-logo.svg
- assets/1220-calendula.svg
- assets/806-clearwell.svg
- assets/jarrell-land.svg
- assets/commercial-portfolio.svg
