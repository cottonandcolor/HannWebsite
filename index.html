About Hannah

A real estate advisor who combines careful analysis with genuinely personal service.

Hannah understands that a real estate decision is rarely only about square footage or price. It may affect a family’s daily life, a business owner’s future or an investor’s long-term plans. Her role is to help clients understand the options, ask the right questions and move forward with confidence.

Before real estate, Hannah studied electrical engineering and computer science and owned a retail business for approximately 15 years. That background supports the organized, analytical and responsive service she brings to every transaction.
